Putin's forces ‘targeting civilians with brutal rigour’: Germany Russian forces in Ukraine are increasingly targeting the civilian population Germany’s foreign minister has alleged. “It is clear to see that this war of aggression by Putin is targeting the civilian population with the most brutal rigour” Annalena Baerbock told reporters in Brussels before a meeting with her European Union counterparts.
