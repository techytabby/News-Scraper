More than 4000 evacuated from front-line cities: Ukraine's deputy PM Ukraine was able to evacuate more than 4000 people from front-line cities on Monday via seven humanitarian corridors its Deputy Prime Minister Iryna Vereshchuk said in a video statement. She said three other humanitarian corridors did not operate successfully and she accused Russian forces of firing on civilians who were evacuating the Kyiv region. Russia has repeatedly denied firing on civilians.
