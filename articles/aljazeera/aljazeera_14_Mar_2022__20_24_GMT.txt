US defence secretary adds Bulgaria to European trip US Defense Secretary Lloyd Austin is adding a stop in Bulgaria to a Europe trip focused on bolstering NATO allies as war rages on in Ukraine the Pentagon has said. Austin will leave on Tuesday for Brussels where he will attend a NATO meeting before travelling to Slovakia. In Bulgaria Austin will meet military and civilian leaders it said without offering further details on his agenda. Bulgaria Moscow’s closest ally during the Cold War is nowa European Union and NATO member state that has condemned Russia's invasion of Ukraine.
