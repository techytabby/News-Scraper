‘What | saw | hope no one will ever see’: Greek diplomat Greece’s consul general in Mariupol the last European Union diplomat to evacuate the besieged Ukrainian port has said it “will become part of a list of cities that were completely destroyed by war”. “What I saw I hope no one will ever see” Manolis Androulakis said as he arrived at Athens International Airport. According to the Greek Foreign Ministry Androulakis assisted dozens of Greek nationals and ethnic Greeks before fleeing Mariupol on Tuesday. After a four-day trip through Ukraine he crossed to Romania through Moldavia along with 10 other Greek nationals.
