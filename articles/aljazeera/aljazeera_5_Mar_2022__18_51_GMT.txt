Zelenskyy makes ‘desperate plea’ to US senators Ukrainian President Zelenskyy made a “desperate plea” to US senators to help his country get more planes to help the country fight the Russian invasion. Zelenskyy made the request on a call joined by more than 300 people including senators some House legislators and aides. Senate Majority Leader Chuck Schumer a Democrat from New York said in a statement that Zelenskyy made a “desperate plea for Eastern European countries to provide Russian-made planes to Ukraine”.
