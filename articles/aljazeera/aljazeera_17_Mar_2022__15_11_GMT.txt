Moscow regrets suspension of Russian- European Mars mission Moscow regrets the “bitter” decision by the European Space Agency to suspend a Russian-European mission to land a rover on Mars over the Kremlin’s Ukraine offensive Russia's space chief has said. “This is a very bitter [decision] for all the enthusiasts of space” Dmitry Rogozin head of Russia’s space agency Roscosmos said on Telegram calling it a “shame”. He added that Moscow will “conduct this research expedition on our own”.
