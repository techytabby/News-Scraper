Baltic nations expel 10 Russian diplomats Latvia Estonia and Lithuania have expelled a total of 10 Russian diplomats the foreign ministries of the three Baltic countries have said. Lithuania expelled four diplomats while Latvia and Estonia each expelled three. The diplomats were expelled in a coordinated move “in connection with activities that are contrary to their diplomatic status and taking into account ongoing Russian aggression in Ukraine” Latvian foreign minister Edgars Rinkevics tweeted.
