Zelenskyy's top economic adviser says war damage tops $100bn Russian forces have so far destroyed at least $100bn worth of infrastructure buildings and other physical assets in Ukraine the chief economic adviser to Ukraine’s president has said. Oleg Ustenko told an online event hosted by the Peterson Institute for International Economics that the war has caused 50 percent of Ukrainian businesses to shut down completely while the other half are operating at well below their capacity.
