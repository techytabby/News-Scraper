Strategic Ukrainian port Mariupol ‘plockaded’: mayor Ukraine’s strategic port city of Mariupol is under a “blockade” by the Russian army after days of “ruthless” attacks its mayor has said calling for the establishment of a humanitarian corridor. “For now we are looking for solutions to humanitarian problems and all possible ways to get Mariupol out of the blockade” said Vadim Boychenko in a message posted on Telegram.
