Bolshoi conductor quits after pressure Tugan Sokhiev the music director and principal conductor at Moscow’s prestigious Bolshoi Theatre has announced his resignation saying he came under pressure to condemn Russia’s invasion of Ukraine. The Russian said in a statement that he was resigning “with immediate effect” from his post at the Moscow theatre as well as his equivalent position at France’s Orchestre National du Capitole de Toulouse.
