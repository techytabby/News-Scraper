Ammonia leak reported at chemicals plant in Sumy A Ukrainian official is raising the alarm over an ammonia leak at a chemicals plant in the northeastern Ukrainian city of Sumy besieged by Russian troops. Zhyvytskyy the Sumy regional governor said the leak was reported at 4:30am local time (02:30 GMT) at the Sumykhimprom plant and that the area within a 5km radius around the plant was hazardous. He did not say what had caused the leak.
