Where is the Russia-Ukraine war heading? Russia’s war on Ukraine has entered a fourth week as increasingly harsh rhetoric from Western powers towards Russian President Vladimir Putin fails to stop attacks in several cities. According to the United Nations more than 600 civilians have died — but the real figure is feared to be higher.
