Protesters briefly seize London mansion linked to Russian oligarch Protesters in London briefly took over a mansion linked to the sanctioned Russian oligarch Oleg Deripaska unfurling the Ukrainian flag as well as a banner stating “This property has been liberated” from a balcony at the building at 5 Belgrave Square. Police said later on Monday that four protesters who gained entry to the building’s balcony “have come down and been arrested”.
