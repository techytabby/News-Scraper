World Bank approves $723m in loans grants for Ukraine The World Bank says its executive board approved a $723m package of loans and grants for Ukraine providing government budget support. The package includes a $350m loan supplement to a prior loan augmented by about $139m through guarantees from the Netherlands and Sweden the bank said in a statement.
