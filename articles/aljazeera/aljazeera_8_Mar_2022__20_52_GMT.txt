US House set to pass Russia sanctions bill Pelosi says Nancy Pelosi speaker of the US House of Representatives has said the chamber is set to pass a Russian sanctions bill later on Tuesday. She said the legislation would support Biden’s decision to ban Russian oil imports. “Our bill has three major provisions: it will ban the import of Russian oil & energy products into the US it will take steps to review Russia’s access to the WTO and explore how we can diminish Russia in the global economy & it will reauthorize & strengthen the Magnitsky Act” Pelosi wrote on Twitter.
