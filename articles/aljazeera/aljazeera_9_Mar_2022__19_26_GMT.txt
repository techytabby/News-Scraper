Russia says it has not breached ceasefire Moscow has rejected accusations that it is breaching an agreed ceasefire to allow the evacuation of Ukrainian civilians. The ceasefire has been strictly observed Colonel-General Mikhail Mizintsev of the Russian Defence Ministry said according to Russia’s TASS news agency. He added Ukraine had shelled Russian positions in the suburbs of Kyiv as well as in Kharkiv Mariupol and Sumy.
