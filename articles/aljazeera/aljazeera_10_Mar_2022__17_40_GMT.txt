Mariupol maternity hospital third to be destroyed: UN The United Nations’ reproductive health agency has said two other maternity hospitals had been attacked and destroyed in Ukraine before Russia’s attack in Mariupol on Wednesday. “In Zhytomyr the maternity [hospital] was completely destroyed. In Saltivsky the maternity [hospital] was also destroyed” Jaime Nadal the Ukraine lead for the UN Population Fund (UNFPA) said in a press conference. The UN official did not specify who launched the attacks on the other hospitals or if there were any victims. The agency estimated about 80000 women will be giving birth during the next three months and more than 4300 women have given birth since Russia's invasion began on February 24.
