US urges Russia to allow civilians to safely depart Ukrainian cities Washington has urged Moscow to allow civilians to safely depart Ukrainian cities and towns besieged by Russian troops saying that the forces now encircle multiple cities after having destroyed much of the critical infrastructure. “Putin’s military plan to quickly capture Ukraine it is clear now has failed. So he’s now turning to a strategy of laying waste to population centers to try to break the will of the people of Ukraine something he will not be able to do” State Department spokesperson Ned Price told reporters.
