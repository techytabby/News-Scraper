Several civilians killed in Makariv air raid emergency services say At least 13 civilians have been killed in an air raid on a bread factory in the town of Makariv on the outskirts of Kyiv according to local emergency services. The services said five people had been rescued from the building’s rubble adding that about 30 people were believed to have been at the factory before the attack.
