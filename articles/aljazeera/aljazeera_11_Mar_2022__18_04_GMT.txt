‘Insane delirium’: Ukraine slams Russian allegations of biological weapons Ukraine’s ambassador to the UN Sergiy Kyslytsya has slammed Russia for convening the Security Council over allegations of biological weapons in Ukraine which Washington London and Kyiv have dismissed as false. “I regret that the Security Council is being now manipulated by the Russian Federation to promote such an insane delirium” Kyslytsya told the Council. “It is an utmost disregard by the Russian Federation [for] the members of [the] Security Council.”
