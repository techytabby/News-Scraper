Russia slams Council of Europe as ‘Russophobic’ A spokeswoman for Russia’s foreign ministry has accused the Council of Europe which expelled Moscow from its ranks earlier this week of being a “Russophobic” instrument serving Western interests. “Due to the Westerners’ Russophobic activity” the Council of Europe is losing its reason for being Maria Zakharova said in a statement. “By placing the service of the bloc’s interests above its own statutory objectives the Council of Europe has been turned into an obedient instrument of the European Union NATO and their satellites” she added.
