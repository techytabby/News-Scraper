Zelenskyy thanks EU leaders for Kyiv visit Zelenskyy has thanked the prime ministers of Poland the Czech Republic and Slovenia who travelled to Kyiv for talks. “These respected men leaders of their beautiful independent European states are not afraid of anything and are more worried about our destiny” Zelenskyy said. The three leaders had gone ahead with the train trip despite worries about the security risks of travelling in a war zone. “With such friends with such countries and neighbours and partners we will be able truly to defeat I don’t want to say who we all know it” he added.
