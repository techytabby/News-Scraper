Putin hails Russia's ‘special operation’ in Ukraine to thousands in packed stadium Russian President Vladimir Putin has hailed what Russia calls its special operation in Ukraine appearing on stage at a rally at Moscow's Luzhniki Stadium in front of thousands of people waving Russian flags. Russia sent tens of thousands of troops into Ukraine on February 24 in an effort to degrade its southern neighbour’s military capabilities and root out people it called dangerous nationalists.
