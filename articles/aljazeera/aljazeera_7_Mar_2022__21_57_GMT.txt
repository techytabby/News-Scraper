Ukrainian civilians must be allowed safe passage: UN aid chief Civilians stuck in areas of active hostilities in Ukraine should be allowed safe passage in any direction they choose UN aid chief Martin Griffiths has said during a UNSC meeting. “The parties must take constant care to spare civilians and civilian homes and infrastructure in their military operations” Griffiths said. Ukrainian cities under attack — including Kharkiv Mariupol and Melitopol — desperately needed aid and medical supplies he added.
