What does neutrality mean and could it lead to peace? For years Ukraine has aspired to join NATO a move that would significantly boost its military in the face of Russian aggression but the chances of membership remain slim even as the war devastates the former Soviet country. Russia refuses Western allegations that it wants to influence Ukraine and claims its main desire is for Ukraine to be neutral a buffer state and out of NATO. Some experts say that Ukraine remaining neutral and out of NATO could be beneficial to regional security. Read more here.
