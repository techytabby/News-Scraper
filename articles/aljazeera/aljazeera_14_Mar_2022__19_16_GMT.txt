Ukraine says it evacuated 548000 people since start of war Ukraine has evacuated 548000 civilians since Russia launched its invasion on February 24 the country’s Deputy Interior Minister Yevheniy Yenin said in a televised interview. “Only in the last 24 hours our emergency rescue crews have attended over 600 calls including over 250 at sites of Russian shelling” he said adding that 50 people had been pulled out from under the rubble of destroyed buildings in this time period.
