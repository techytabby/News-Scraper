UK to deploy Sky Sabre missile defence system in Poland: Minister Britain will deploy its Sky Sabre missile system in Poland its defence minister said during a visit to Warsaw as NATO moves to beef up the security of its eastern flank in view of Russia’s invasion of Ukraine. “We are going to deploy the Sky Sabre medium-range anti-air missile system to Poland with about 100 personnel to make sure that we stand alongside Poland protecting her airspace from any further aggression by Russia” Ben Wallace told a news conference.
