France doesn't rule out energy sanctions against Russia Sanctions against Russia over its war in Ukraine are hurting its economy and Russia’s president France’s finance minister said adding that banning Russian oil and gas imports into the European Union remained an option for Paris. “They're hurting the Russian state and they're hurting Vladimir Putin” Bruno Le Maire told LCI television in an interview. “Should we in the immediate stop buying Russian oil should a little bit further down the line we stop importing Russian gas? The president has never ruled out these options” he said.
