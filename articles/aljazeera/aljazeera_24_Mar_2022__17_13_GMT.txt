Scholz warns Moscow not to use chemical weapons in Ukraine German Chancellor Olaf Scholz has warned Russia against using chemical weapons in Ukraine. “Should Russia be preparing a false-flag operation in order to use such [chemical and biological] weapons [in Ukraine] this would be in violation of all rules agreements and conventions” he told journalists in Brussels. “It is our concern not only to point this out but also to warn in the conversations we have: Don’t do it” he added.
