Russia to supply Belarus with modern weapons: Belta news agency The leaders of Russia and Belarus have agreed that Moscow would supply its neighbour with the most up-to-date military equipment in the near future the official Belarus Belta news agency said. Belta also said that Russian President Vladimir Putin and Belarus leader Alexander Lukashenko agreed at their Kremlin meeting on joint steps for mutual support in the face of Western sanctions including on energy prices. The news agency did not provide additional details.
