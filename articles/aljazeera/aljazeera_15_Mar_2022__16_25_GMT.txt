Photos: Residential areas hit in Kyiv leaving at least four dead Raids on residential areas in Kyiv have killed at least four people emergency services said as Russian troops intensified their attacks on the Ukrainian capital. Bodies were pulled from the rubble after an attack on a 16-storey building in the Sviatoshynsky district the emergency service said in a Facebook post on Tuesday adding that 27 people had been rescued from the site.
