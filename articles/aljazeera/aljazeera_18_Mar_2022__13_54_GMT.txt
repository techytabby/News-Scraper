Fearing front-line deployment some Russians resist conscription As rumours of martial law spread across Russia in early March some young men abruptly left their homeland fearing they would be conscripted and sent to the battlefield in Ukraine. They are among thousands of people who have fled Russia since February 24 as a crackdown grows on anti-war sentiment. Read more here.
