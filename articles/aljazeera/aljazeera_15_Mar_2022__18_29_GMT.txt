Prime ministers of Poland Czech Republic and Slovenia arrive in Kyiv The prime ministers of Poland the Czech Republic and Slovenia have arrived in Kyiv according to Polish Prime Minister Mateusz Morawiecki. “We must stop the tragedy that is happening in the East as soon as possible” Morawiecki wrote on Facebook. “This is why together with (Polish) Deputy Prime Minister Jaroslaw Kaczynski and Prime Ministers Petr Fiala and Janez Jansa we are in Kyiv.” The visit was aimed at confirming the support of the European Union for Ukraine and to present a broad package of support for the country.
