Toll from attack on Chernihiv bread line rises to 13 The number of civilians killed in an alleged Russian attack on a bread shop in the city of Chernihiv has risen to 13 according to Ukrainian state news agency Ukrinform. The outlet quoted a local police official Volodymyr Nidzelsky saying that “13 civilians and dozens of people were injured” in the attack.
