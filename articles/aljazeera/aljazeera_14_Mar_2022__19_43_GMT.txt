Ukraine's economy could collapse if war drags on: IMF The Russian invasion could plunge Ukraine into a devastating recession the International Monetary Fund (IMF) has said. Ata minimum the country would see “output falling 10 percent this year assuming a prompt resolution of the war” the IMF said. The lender added that if the conflict is prolonged the situation will worsen. Citing wartime data for conflicts in Iraq Lebanon Syria and Yemen it predicted the “annual output contraction could eventually be much higher in the range of 25 to 35 percent.”
