‘Very big gap’ remains in peace talks between Russia and Ukraine Ukraine and Russia are taking peace talks seriously but a very big gap remains between the two sides Western officials have said. “Both sides are taking them seriously but there is a very very big gap between the positions in question” one official said speaking on condition of anonymity. “Those ... who saw [Russian] President [Vladimir] Putin addressing the nation yesterday would be forgiven for thinking that Russia was not in [a] compromising mood” another official said.
