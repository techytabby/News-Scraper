Three killed in attack on Mariupol hospital local officials say At least three people including one child were killed when Russia bombed a children’s hospital in Mariupol local officials have said. “Three people were killed including a female child in yesterday's attack on a children’s and maternity hospital in Ukraine’s besieged Mariupol according to updated figures this morning” Mariupol’s city council said on the Telegram messaging app. Russia has refuted Ukrainian claims that the site was still functioning as a hospital arguing it had been taken over by troops.
