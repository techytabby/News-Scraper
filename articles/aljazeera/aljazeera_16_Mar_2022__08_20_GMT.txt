Prosecutor general: 103 children have been killed in war in Ukraine The Ukrainian prosecutor general has said that 103 children have been killed so far in the war in Ukraine. Russian forces have struck more than 400 educational establishments and 59 of them have been destroyed Prosecutor General Iryna Venediktova said on Facebook.
