Ukraine accuses Russia of deliberately targeting residential areas Sergiy Kyslytsya Ukraine’s ambassador to the UN has accused Russia of ‘deliberately’ targeting residential areas in the country. “A map seized by the Ukrainian troops from Russian prisoners of war confirms that bombardments of residential areas had been planned in advance” Kyslytsya told the Security Council. “Captured Russian pilots at today’s press conference in Kyiv have reconfirmed that they deliberately dropped the bombs on residential areas.”
