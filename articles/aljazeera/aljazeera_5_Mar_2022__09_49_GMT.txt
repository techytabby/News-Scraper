US Germany Netherlands to deploy more military in Lithuania: Defence minister Germany will deploy air defence capabilities to Lithuania and the US will send a troop battalion armed with tanks Lithuania's defence minister has said. The deployments which are also due to include more troops from the Netherlands are separate to troops coming for military exercises Lithuania is set to host in March Arvydas Anusauskas told reporters. The deployments and exercises will increase the total number of foreign NATO troops in Lithuania to 4000 by March-end from 3000 now the minister said. Lithuania had asked NATO to add more troops and equipment following Russia's invasion of Ukraine. [RUSSIA-UKRAINE CRISIS) Blocking access to Facebook
