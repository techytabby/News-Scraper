UN health agency verifies attacks on 18 health facilities The World Health Organization (WHO) says it has verified 18 attacks on healthcare facilities and personnel in Ukraine resulting in 10 deaths and 16 injuries. WHO Health Emergencies Programme Director Michael Ryan warned that the violence was creating a health crisis that could worsen as some 1000 hospitals and health clinics were situated within 10km of the front lines. “The health system is becoming engulfed in this conflict engulfed in this crisis” Ryan told a press conference.
