Polish Czech and Slovenian PMs return to Poland after Kyiv trip Delegations including the prime ministers of Poland the Czech Republic and Slovenia have arrived in Poland after their visit to Kyiv a Polish government spokesperson has said. “The Polish Slovenian and Czech delegations safely returned from Kyiv to Poland” Piotr Muller wrote on Twitter. Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy and Prime Minister Denys Shmyhal Polish Prime Minister Mateusz Morawiecki and Deputy Prime Minister Jaroslaw Kaczynski Czech Prime Minister Petr Fiala and Slovenian Prime Minister Janez Jansa met in Kyiv on Tuesday [Ukrainian Presidential Press Service/Handout via Reuters]
