US launches task force to pursue ‘corrupt Russian oligarchs’ US Attorney General Merrick Garland has announced the launch of a task force to pursue “corrupt Russian oligarchs” and violators of sanctions imposed on Russia for its invasion of Ukraine. “The Justice Department will use all of its authorities to seize the assets of individuals and entities who violate these sanctions” Garland said in a statement announcing the launch of ‘Task Force KleptoCapture.” Read more here.
