Italy prepares plan to take in 175000 Ukrainian refugees Italy has drawn up plans to take in up to 175000 Ukrainian refugees a draft decree seen by the Reuters news agency has said. The plan is expected to be approved by the cabinet later on Friday. Some 53600 Ukrainians including 27000 women and 21600 children have come to Italy so far following Russia’s invasion of their country on February 24 interior ministry data shows.
