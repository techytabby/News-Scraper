Russia lost 14000 servicemen: Ukraine's defence ministry Ukraine’s defence ministry says that Russia has lost some 14000 servicemen including about 200 in the past 24 hours. It also said that Ukrainian forces destroyed 444 Russian tanks 1435 armoured vehicles 86 planes 108 helicopters 11 drones and three ships. It said the figures were approximate and their verification was “complicated” by the high intensity of the fighting. Russia’s defence ministry has reported the death toll only on March 2 saying that 498 servicemen had been killed in Ukraine. There is no way to verify the claims of both sides.
