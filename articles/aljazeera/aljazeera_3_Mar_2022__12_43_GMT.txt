Mariupol's mayor claims Russian forces are trying to blockade the city Russian forces are trying to create a blockade around Mariupol and are attacking rail links to prevent civilians from evacuating the key port city its mayor has said. “The invaders are systematically and methodically trying to blockade the city of Mariupol” Vadym Boichenko said in a video broadcast. Constant attacks over the past 24 hours have cut off water and power supplies and the local authorities need a ceasefire to restore power he added.
