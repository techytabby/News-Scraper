Biden to speak with Xi The presidents of the United State and China will speak at 9am Eastern time (13:00 GMT) on Friday the White House says. “This is part of ongoing efforts to maintain open lines of communication between the US and the PRC [People’s Republic of China]” it said in a statement. “The two leaders will discuss managing the competition between the two countries as well as Russia’s war against Ukraine and other issues  0 of mutual concern.”
