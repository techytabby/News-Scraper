UK says Russia attacked western Ukraine cities The United Kingdom has said Russian air and missile forces had conducted raids in the past 24 hours against the western Ukrainian cities of Lutsk and Ivano-Frankivsk. Russian tactical aircraft supporting the advance of Russian ground forces were primarily relying on unguided “dumb” munitions the UK Ministry of Defence intelligence update posted on Twitter said.
