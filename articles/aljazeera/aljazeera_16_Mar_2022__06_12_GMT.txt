What happens if Russia defaults on its debt? Russia's economy is in dire straits and on Wednesday it faces its first payment on US dollar bonds since it invaded Ukraine last month. Moscow is due to pay $117m in interest on two dollar-denominated sovereign bonds or risk defaulting on its debt.
