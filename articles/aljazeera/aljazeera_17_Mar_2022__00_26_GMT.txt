UK warns Russia ‘likely resorting to less precise weapons’ The British Ministry of Defence says Russia’s failure to control Ukrainian airspace means it has probably expended far more stand-off air-launched weapons than originally planned. “As a result it is likely Russia is resorting to the use of older less precise weapons which are less militarily effective and more likely to result in civilian casualties” the ministry said in its latest intelligence update.
