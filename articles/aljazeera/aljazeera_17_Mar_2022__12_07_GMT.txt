Ukraine parliamentarian says 130 rescued from Mariupol theatre Some 130 civilians have so far been rescued from the bombed-out theatre in Mariupol according to a Ukrainian parliamentarian. “Good news that we need so urgently. The air raid shelter under the theatre of Mariupol has stood up to it. Around 130 people have already been saved” Olga Stefanishyna wrote on Facebook. “It’s a miracle” she said describing how rescue workers are removing rubble to free other survivors.
