Qatar calls on all parties to ‘exercise restraint’ over Ukraine Qatar's foreign minister has called on all parties to “exercise restraint” and to avoid further escalation over Ukraine in a phone call with his Ukrainian counterpart according to a Qatari foreign ministry statement. Sheikh Mohammed bin Abdulrahman bin Jassim Al Thani told Ukraine’s Dmytro Kuleba that Qatar urged “all parties to exercise restraint resolve disputes through constructive dialogue and diplomatic methods and to settle international disputes by peaceful means” the statement said.
