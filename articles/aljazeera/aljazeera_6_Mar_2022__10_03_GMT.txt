More than 1.5 million refugees flee Ukraine in past 10 days: UN The number of people fleeing Russia’s invasion of Ukraine has topped 1.5 million making it Europe’s “fastest growing refugee crisis” since World War II the United Nations has said. “More than 1.5 million refugees from Ukraine have crossed into neighbouring countries in 10 days — the fastest growing refugee crisis in Europe since World War II” it said in a statement on Twitter.
