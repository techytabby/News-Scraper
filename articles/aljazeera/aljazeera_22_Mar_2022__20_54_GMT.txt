Russia would only use nuclear weapons in case of ‘existential threat’: Kremlin Russia's security policy dictates that the country would only use nuclear weapons if its very existence were threatened Peskov has told US broadcaster CNN. The Kremlin spokesman made the comment in an English-language interview when asked whether he was confident that the Russian president would not use nuclear weapons. “We have a concept of domestic security and it’s public you can read all the reasons for nuclear arms to be used. So if it is an existential threat for our country then it [the nuclear arsenal] can be used in accordance with our concept” he said. “There are no other reasons that were mentioned in that text.”
