Foreign students fleeing Russia's war on Ukraine hope to return In the early morning in Zahony a Hungarian town near the Ukraine border trains arrive every few hours from the Ukrainian city of Chop. Among those disembarking from the dark blue trains are hundreds of students many of them from countries such as Nigeria India Egypt and Morocco who have spent recent years studying in Ukraine.
