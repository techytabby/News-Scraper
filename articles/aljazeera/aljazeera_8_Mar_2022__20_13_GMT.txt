Russia announces ceasefire for Wednesday morning Moscow has announced a humanitarian ceasefire in Ukraine for Wednesday morning to carry out the evacuation of civilians from five cities Russian news agencies reported. “From 10:00 MSK (07:00 GMT) on March 9 2022 the Russian Federation is declaring a ‘regime of silence’ and is ready to provide humanitarian corridors” TASS news agency said quoting a department of the Russian defence ministry charged with humanitarian operations in Ukraine. Information about corridors from Kyiv Chernihiv Sumy Kharkiv and Mariupol will be sent to Ukrainian Deputy Prime Minister Iryna Vereshchuk.
