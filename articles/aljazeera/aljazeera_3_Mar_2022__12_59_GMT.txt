Central bank governor says Ukraine's financial system remains resilient Ukraine’s central bank governor Kyrylo Shevchenko says the country’s banking and financial system has remained resilient and been boosted by international financial support amounting to around $15bn. “The national bank is doing everything necessary to ensure the continuity of both non-cash and cash payments and the smooth functioning of the banking system of the state under martial law” Shevchenko said in a news briefing.
