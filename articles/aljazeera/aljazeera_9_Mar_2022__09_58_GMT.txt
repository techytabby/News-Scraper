Russia will render Ukraine neutral foreign ministry spokeswoman says Moscow will achieve its goal of ensuring Ukraine is neutral but would prefer to do that through talks rather than conflict a spokeswoman for Russia’s foreign ministry has said. Maria Zakharova told reporters at a media briefing that “some progress” had been made in three rounds of negotiations with Ukrainian officials. She said the Kremlin’s aims do not include overthrowing the Kyiv government and added that Russia's military operation was proceeding as planned so far. In wide-ranging remarks Zakharova also went on to accuse the US-led NATO military alliance of pursuing a “confrontational course” by building up forces along its eastern flank.
