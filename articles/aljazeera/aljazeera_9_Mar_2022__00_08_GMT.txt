Americans donating body armour ammunition A Texas marketing executive is among several American donors shipping body armour to Ukrainians through relief groups in the US. Bret Starr told Reuters news agency he expects to send 20 sets of helmets and bullet-proof vests through the Ukranian American Coordinating Council this week followed by up to 2000 more through cash donations and gear. In New York state the Suffolk County Sheriff's Office has given around 450 pieces of body armour to the Long Island-Ukraine Emergency Response Drive.
