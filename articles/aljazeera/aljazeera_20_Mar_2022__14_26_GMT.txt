Ukraine accuses Russian forces of attacking retirement home in Luhansk region Ukraine’s human rights ombudsman Lyudmila Denisova has accused Russian forces of shooting dozens of people at a home for the elderly in the Luhansk region. “Today it became known about another terrible crime against humanity committed by the racist occupation forces — the shooting of 56 elderly people in Luhansk region” Denisova said on Telegram. According to the ombudsman Russian troops fired from a tank in the town of Kreminna on March 11 killing 56 people. Fifteen survivors were abducted by the occupiers and taken to the occupied territory in Svatove. Denisova did not provide evidence for the attack in the statement.
