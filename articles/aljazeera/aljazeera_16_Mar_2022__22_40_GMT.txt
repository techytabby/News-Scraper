Ukraine swapped nine Russian soldiers to free detained mayor: Interfax Ukraine has handed over nine captured Russian soldiers to secure the freedom of the mayor of the southeastern city of Melitopol who was detained last week the Interfax Ukraine news agency has reported. The office of President Zelenskyy earlier said mayor Ivan Fedorov had been released but gave no details. “Ivan Fedorov was released from Russian captivity ... For him Russia received nine captured soldiers who were born in 2002 and 2003. These are actually children” Interfax Ukraine quoted Zelenskyy’s press aide Darya Zarivnaya as saying.
