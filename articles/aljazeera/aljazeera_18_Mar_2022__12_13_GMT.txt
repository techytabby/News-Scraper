Ukraine will not give up EU bid as compromise to Russia says Ukrainian official Ukraine will not abandon its bid to join the EU to reach a compromise with Russia as part of an agreement to end the war Zelenskyy’s deputy chief of staff has said. “I will be categorical this is absolutely unacceptable. It is our choice the application for EU membership has been submitted and now it is being put into practice” deputy chief of staff Andrii Sybiha said. Speaking on national television Sybiha said negotiations with Russia were continuing but difficult.
