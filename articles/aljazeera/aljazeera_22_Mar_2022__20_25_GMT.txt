Russia would only use nuclear weapons if its existence were threatened: Kremlin Kremlin spokesman Dmitry Peskov has told CNN in an interview that Russia would only use nuclear weapons if its very existence were threatened. “We have a concept of domestic security and it’s public you can read all the reasons for nuclear arms to be used. So if it is an existential threat for our country then it [the nuclear arsenal] can be used in accordance with our concept” he said.
