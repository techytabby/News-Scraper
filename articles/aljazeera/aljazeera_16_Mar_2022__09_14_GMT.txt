Russian shelling kills one in Kharkiv Russian shelling has killed one man in Kharkiv and damaged two apartment buildings according to the emergencies ministry. However the shelling subsided significantly last night a Kharkiv resident said. “The night was relatively calm we even could get some sleep” a mother of two who has been holed up in her apartment in central Kharkiv told Al Jazeera. The windows and the balcony doors of her apartment were destroyed by shelling earlier this month and patched with plastic and blankets and the family spends nights on the bathroom floor she said.
