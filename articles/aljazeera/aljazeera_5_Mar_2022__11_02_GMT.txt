Mariupol evacuation on hold Mariupol officials say they are delaying the evacuation of civilians accusing Russian troops of breaking the ceasefire. “Due to the fact that the Russian side does not adhere to the ceasefire and has continued shelling both of Mariupol itself and its environs and for security reasons the evacuation of the civilian population has been postponed” city officials said in a statement on social media. There was no immediate comment by the Russian army.
