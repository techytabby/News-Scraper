Russian missiles hit area near airport in Lviv: Mayor Russian missiles have struck an area near the airport of Lviv its mayor Andriy Sadovy has said though he added that the airport itself had not been attacked. Writing on messaging app Telegram Sadovy said he could not give a precise address of the targeted area ” but it’s definitely not an airport”.
