Putin blames Ukraine for failed civilian evacuations from Mariupol Ina telephone call with his French counterpart Macron Putin has blamed Kyiv for failed civilian evacuations from Mariupol. Putin “drew attention to the fact that Kyiv still does not fulfil agreements reached on this acute humanitarian issue” according to a statement from the Kremlin after two agreements to evacuate Mariupol fell though following allegations of ceasefire breaches.
