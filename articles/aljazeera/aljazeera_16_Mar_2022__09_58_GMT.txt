Lavrov sees ‘some hope’ in Ukraine talks Lavrov sees “some hope” for reaching a compromise in the negotiations between Moscow and Kyiv to end the war according to local media. The Russian foreign minister told the RBK newspaper that he believed there were already positions upon which the sides “are close to agreement”. While the talks are difficult “there is some hope of reaching a compromise” he said. Lavrov said Ukraine’s political and military neutrality in exchange for security guarantees from Moscow were now being “seriously discussed”.
