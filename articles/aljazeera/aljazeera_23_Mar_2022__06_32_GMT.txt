Russia's Indonesia envoy: Putin plans to attend G20 summit Putin intends to attend a Group of 20 (G20) summit being hosted by Indonesia later this year Russia’s ambassador in Jakarta has said following calls by some bloc members for the country to be barred from the group. “Not only G20 many organisations are trying to expel Russia ... the reaction of the West is absolutely disproportional” Ambassador Lyudmila Vorobieva told a news conference.
