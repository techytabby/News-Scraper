Biden-Xi call was ‘direct’ and ‘substantive’: US official The phone call between Biden and China’s Xi lasted about two hours and was “direct” and “substantive” a senior US official has said. “I would say the conversation was direct. It was substantive and it was detailed” the official who spoke to reporters on condition of anonymity said. “The two leaders spent the preponderance of their time discussing Russia’s unprovoked and unjustified invasion of Ukraine as well as the implications of the crisis for US-China relations and the international order.”
