Russian gov't sites facing ‘unprecedented’ cyberattacks Russian government websites are facing unprecedented cyberattacks and efforts are being made to filter foreign web traffic the TASS news agency cited the digital ministry as saying. Russian government entities and state-owned companies have been targeted over events in Ukraine with the websites of the Kremlin flagship carrier Aeroflot and major lender Sberbank among those to have seen outages or temporary access issues in recent weeks.
