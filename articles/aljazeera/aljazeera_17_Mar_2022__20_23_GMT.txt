12 dead in dozens of attacks on Ukrainian health care: WHO The World Health Organization has verified 43 attacks on health care in Ukraine WHO Director-General Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus told the UN Security Council. The attacks have resulted in the deaths of 12 people and injured dozens more. “In any conflict attacks on health care are a violation of international humanitarian law” Tedros told the 15-member council without specifying who was to blame for the attacks.
