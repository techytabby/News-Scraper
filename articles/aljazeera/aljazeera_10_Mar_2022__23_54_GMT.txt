Situation in besieged city of Mariupol ‘a total horror’ Maria Moskaleno who managed to escape Mariupol last week says her parents remain trapped there. “It’s a total horror it’s a humanitarian catastrophe” Moskaleno told Al Jazeera. “They don’t know will they have food till the end of the blockade.” According to Moskaleno her parents have been cooking food “on the streets” with branches of wood from surrounding trees. “It’s a disaster it’s really scary ... Russians are constantly bombing ... constantly rockets are flying around them they're really scared they just don’t have hope of salvation” she added.
