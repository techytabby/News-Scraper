WHO warns of mental health crisis among Ukrainian refugees About half a million refugees from Ukraine who fled to Poland need support for mental health disorders and 30000 have severe mental health problems WHO’s representative in the country has said. Those arriving in Poland are suffering from a range of health problems including diarrhoea and dehydration but the main need among those who have fled Ukraine is for support due to trauma Paloma Cuchi told a briefing in Geneva.
