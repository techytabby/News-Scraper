US imposes more sanctions on Putin's inner circle Russian oligarchs The US has announced more sanctions on members of Russian President Vladimir Putin’s inner circle as well as oligarchs and others who backed Russia’s invasion of Ukraine. The Treasury Department said in a statement that three family members of Kremlin Spokesperson Dmitry Peskov were targeted by the sanctions as was the management board of the sanctioned VIB Bank. Twelve members of the Russian Duma were also blacklisted the department said.
