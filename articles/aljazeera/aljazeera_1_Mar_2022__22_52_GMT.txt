World Bank preparing $3bn aid package for Ukraine The World Bank says it is preparing a $3bn aid package for Ukraine which will include at least $350m in immediate funds. The first tranche of the aid “will be submitted to the Board for approval this week followed by $200m in fast-disbursing support for health and education” the World Bank said in a joint statement with the International Monetary Fund (IMF). The IMF will consider the country’s latest request for emergency financing “as early as next week.”
