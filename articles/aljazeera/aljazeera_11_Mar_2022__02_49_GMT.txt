WHO says it advised Ukraine to destroy pathogens in health labs to prevent disease spread The World Health Organization (WHO) advised Ukraine to destroy high-threat pathogens housed in the country’s public health laboratories to prevent “any potential spills” that would spread disease among the population the agency has told Reuters.  wR In response to questions from the news agency about its work with Ukraine ahead of and during Russia’s invasion WHO said in an email that it has collaborated with Ukrainian public health labs for several years to promote security practices that help prevent “accidental or deliberate release of pathogens”. “As part of this work WHO has strongly recommended to the Ministry of Health in Ukraine and other responsible bodies to destroy high- threat pathogens to prevent any potential spills” the UN agency said.
