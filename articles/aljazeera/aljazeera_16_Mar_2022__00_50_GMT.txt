Ukraine says its forces repelled Russian attack on Kharkiv Ukrainian forces have repelled an attack on Kharkiv by Russian troops who tried to storm the city from their positions in Piatykhatky a suburb 15 kilometres (9 miles) to the north according to the head of the Kharkiv region. The Ukrainian army was able “to push the enemy back beyond its previous position” Oleh Synehubov said on the messaging app Telegram. He called it a “shameful defeat”.
