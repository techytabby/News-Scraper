Lavrov and Kuleba begin talks in Turkey Lavrov and Kuleba have begun talks the spokeswoman for Russia’s foreign ministry has said. “The meeting has started” Zakharova told reporters. The pair’s meeting which marks the first high-level contact between Kyiv and Moscow since Russia launched its invasion on February 24 was being brokered by Turkish Foreign Minister Mevlut Cavusoglu.
