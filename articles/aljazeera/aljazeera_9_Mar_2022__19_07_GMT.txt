Russia-Ukraine war military dispatch: March 9 2022 Russian forces are moving albeit slowly towards Izyum southeast of Kharkiv while also trying to lock Chernihiv north of the capital Kyiv from all sides. At the same time they are advancing north of Mykolaiv after failed attempts against the southern city and are trying to cut west of Kyiv in order to encircle it. Meanwhile Belarusians living in Ukraine’s capital have reportedly created a separate battalion named after 19th-century revolutionary Kastus Kalinouski to defend Kyiv. Read the story here.
