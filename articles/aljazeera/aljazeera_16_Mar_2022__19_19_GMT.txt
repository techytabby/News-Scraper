Biden calls Putin ‘a war criminal’ Biden has called Putin “a war criminal” in the sharpest condemnation yet of Russian actions by a US official since the invasion of Ukraine. The White House had been hesitant to declare Putin’s actions those of a war criminal saying it was a legal term that required research. But in a speech in which he pledged more aid to help Ukraine fight Russia the US president said Russian troops had bombed hospitals and held doctors hostage.
