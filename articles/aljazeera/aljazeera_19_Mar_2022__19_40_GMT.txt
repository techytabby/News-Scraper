More than 6000 people evacuated: Ukraine official The deputy head of the president’s office has said a total of 6623 people were evacuated from Ukrainian cities through humanitarian corridors on Saturday. In an online post Kyrylo Tymoshenko said 4128 people had left the port city of Mariupol. On Friday Tymoshenko said 9145 people had managed to leave cities across Ukraine during the day.
