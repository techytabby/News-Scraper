Russia's largest lender Sberbank quits Europe Russia’s largest lender Sberbank has said it is quitting the European market after coming under pressure from Western sanctions levelled against the state bank. “Tn the current environment Sberbank has decided to withdraw from the European market” the lender said in a statement carried by Russian news agencies. The bank’s European subsidiaries were facing “abnormal cash outflows and threats to the safety of employees and branches” the statement said.
