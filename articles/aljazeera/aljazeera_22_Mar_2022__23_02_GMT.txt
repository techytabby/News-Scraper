US EU slam new sentencing of Kremlin critic Navalny The US and EU have slammed a new sentence handed down to jailed Kremlin critic Alexey Navalny calling it a “sham” and another example of Russia’s widening crackdown on dissenting voices amid its invasion of Ukraine. A Russian court on Tuesday sentenced Navalny to nine additional years in a maximum-security prison on fraud and contempt of court charges as well as a fine of 1.2 million roubles ($11500).
