Russia recruiting Syrians to fight in Ukraine: Pentagon Russia is recruiting Syrian and other foreign fighters as it ramps up its assault on Ukraine the Pentagon says. Moscow entered the Syrian civil war in 2015 on the side of President Bashar al-Assad’s regime and now US Department of Defense officials say Putin is “on a recruiting mission” to bring some of those fighters into Ukraine according to the Wall Street Journal. “We do believe that the accounts of them — the Russians — seeking Syrian fighters to augment their forces in Ukraine we believe there’s truth to that” Pentagon spokesman John Kirby told journalists when asked about the reports.
