Ukraine Russia begin third round of talks The third round of talks between Ukrainian and Russian delegations has started. Moments before the negotiations began Ukrainian presidential adviser Mykhailo Podolyak urged Moscow to halt Russian forces’ “large-scale violence against civilians”. “In a few minutes we will start talking to representatives of a country that seriously believes large-scale violence against civilians is an argument. Prove that this is not the case” Podolyak tweeted.
