Ukraine's civilian toll 227 dead 525 injured: UN The UN human rights office says 227 civilians have been killed and 525 others injured in Ukraine from the start of the Russian assault to midnight March 1. “Most of these casualties were caused by the use of explosive weapons with a wide impact area including shelling from heavy artillery and multi-launch rocket systems and air strikes” it said in a statement issued in Geneva. It added that the true toll is believed to be “considerably higher” due to reporting delays in areas where intensive hostilities have taken place.
