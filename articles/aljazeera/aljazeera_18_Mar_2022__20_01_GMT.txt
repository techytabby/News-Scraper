Russia calls Council of Europe a ‘Russophobic’ tool of the West Russia’s outspoken foreign ministry spokeswoman Maria Zakharova has accused the Council of Europe of being a “Russophobic” instrument serving Western interests. The pan-European rights body expelled Russia on Wednesday after more than a quarter of a century of membership. “Due to the Westerners’ Russophobic activity” the Council of Europe is losing its reason for being Zakharova said in a statement. “By placing the service of the bloc’s interests above its own statutory objectives the Council of Europe has been turned into an obedient instrument of the European Union NATO and their satellites” she added.
