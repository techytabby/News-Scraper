Russian-European Mars mission suspended over Ukraine war: ESA A Russian-European mission to land a rover on Mars has been suspended due to the sanctions over Moscow’s invasion of Ukraine and its “tragic consequences” the European Space Agency (ESA) said. “We deeply deplore the human casualties and tragic consequences of the aggression towards Ukraine” the agency said as it confirmed the suspension of the ExoMars mission.
