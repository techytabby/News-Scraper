China stands on ‘right side of history’ on Ukraine war: Wang Yi China’s Foreign Minister Wang Yi has said Beijing stands on the right side of history over the crisis in Ukraine and its position is “in line with the wishes of most countries”. “China will never accept any external coercion or pressure and opposes any unfounded accusations and suspicious against China” Wang told reporters on Saturday evening. “We have always stood for maintaining peace and opposing war” Wang said. “China’s position is objective and fair and is in line with the wishes of most countries. Time will prove that China’s claims are on the right side of history.”
