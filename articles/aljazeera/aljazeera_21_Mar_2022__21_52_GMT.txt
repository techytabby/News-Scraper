US-Russia tensions may hinder issues beyond Ukraine: AJE correspondent The spike in tensions between the US and Russia may affect the Biden administration’s policy goals beyond Ukraine including efforts to revive the Iran nuclear deal Al Jazeera’s Kimberly Halkett reporting from Washington has said. “The fact that the US ambassador has been summoned in Russia — and the fact that ties are not only strained but have the potential to be severed completely — could put a number of US policy objectives at risk” Halkett said.
