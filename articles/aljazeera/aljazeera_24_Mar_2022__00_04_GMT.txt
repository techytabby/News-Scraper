US making contingency plans in case Russia uses chemical or nuclear weapons: NYT The New York Times says the White House has assembled a group of national security officials to draw up plans for how the US and its allies should respond if Putin orders the use of chemical biological or nuclear weapons in Ukraine. Known as the Tiger Team the group is also looking at responses if the Russian president reaches into NATO territory to attack convoys bringing weapons and aid to Ukraine the Times reported citing several officials involved in the process.
