Two Ukrainian servicemen killed in Lutsk airfield attack official says Russian air raids on an airfield in Lutsk killed two Ukrainian servicemen and wounded six other people the head of the surrounding Volyn region Yuriy Pohulyayko has said. Pohulyayko said on the Telegram messaging app that four rockets had been fired at the site by a Russian bomber at about 5:45am local time (03:45 GMT).
