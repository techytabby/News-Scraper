German broadcasters ARD ZDF stop reporting from Russia: Statement German public broadcasters ARD and ZDF have said they were stopping their reporting from Russia after Moscow announced its new legislation allowing jail terms on media outlets publishing “false information” about the military. The broadcasters will “suspend their reporting from their Moscow bureau for the time being” they said in a statement following similar decisions by other news media including BBC News and Bloomberg News.
