Mayor of Ukraine's Kharkiv says Russian forces firing at city non-stop The mayor of Ukraine’s front line city of Kharkiv has said the city had been under constant attack by Russian forces who have fired at central districts causing an unspecified number of casualties. “They're firing at us constantly” Mayor Thor Terekhoy told national television.
