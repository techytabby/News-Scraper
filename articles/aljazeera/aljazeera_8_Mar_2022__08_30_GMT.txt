UN warns second wave of refugees will be more vulnerable The UN refugee agency (UNHCR) has warned a second wave of refugees from Ukraine will follow the first mass exodus from the country with those escaping later likely to be even more vulnerable. “If the war continues we will start seeing people that have no resources and no connections” UNHCR head Filippo Grandi told a news conference. “That will be a more complex situation to manage for European countries going forward and there will need to be even more solidarity by everybody in Europe and beyond” he added.
