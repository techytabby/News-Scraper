Biden expected to sign bill containing $13.6bn in Ukraine aid US President Joe Biden is set to sign a spending bill that contains $13.6bn in emergency military and humanitarian aid to Ukraine. The US Senate gave final approval to the bill late on Thursday sending it to Biden’s desk for final approval. “We're giving the Ukrainians billions for food medicine shelter and support for the over two million refugees who have had to leave Ukraine” said Senate Majority Leader Chuck Schumer.
