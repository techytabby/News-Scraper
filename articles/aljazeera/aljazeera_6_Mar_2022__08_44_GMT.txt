Russia: Ukrainian military airbase hit with long-range weapons Russia's defence ministry says it struck and disabled Ukraine’s Starokostiantyniv military airbase with long-range high-precision weapons. Russian defence ministry spokesman Igor Konashenkov said: “On the morning of March 6 strikes were carried out by high-precision long- range weapons. The Ukrainian air force base near Starokostiantyniv was disabled.” He said a Ukrainian-controlled S-300 missile system had also been destroyed and that Russia downed 10 Ukrainian planes and helicopters over the past 24 hours.
