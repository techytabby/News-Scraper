How much oil does the US get from Russia? The US imports Russian oil but it is not highly dependent on the country for its supplies. In 2021 the US imported an average of 209000 barrels per day (bpd) of crude oil and 500000 bpd of other petroleum products from Russia according to the American Fuel and Petrochemical Manufacturers (AFPM) trade association. This represented 3 percent of US crude oil imports and one percent of the total crude oil processed by US refineries. Read the story here.
