Ukraine presidential adviser: Over 2500 Mariupol residents killed More than 2500 residents of the Black Sea port city of Mariupol have been killed since Russia invaded Ukraine on February 24 presidential adviser Oleksiy Arestovych said in a televised interview. He said he was citing figures from the Mariupol city administration and accused Russian forces of preventing humanitarian aid reaching the encircled city. Russia says it does not target civilians.
