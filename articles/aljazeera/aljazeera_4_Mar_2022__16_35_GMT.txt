EU wants Ukraine membership ‘as soon as possible’: deputy chief Ukraine should become a member of the European Union “as soon as possible” the deputy head of the EU Commission has said. “Now it is time for a strong message. It is time for signaling that the Ukrainian people are one of the European peoples. We want them in as soon as possible” Commission Vice-President Maros Sefcovic said after a meeting of EU ministers in Arles in the south of France.
