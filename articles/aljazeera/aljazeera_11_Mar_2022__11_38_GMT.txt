No real Russians ashamed of Ukraine conflict Kremlin claims Russians who say they are ashamed of the country’s “special military operation” in Ukraine are not real Russians the Kremlin has said. “A real Russian is never ashamed to be Russian” Kremlin spokesman Peskov told reporters during a news briefing when asked about a slogan — ‘ashamed to be Russian’ — that emerged in the wake of Moscow’s incursion in Ukraine. “If someone says such things then they are just not Russian” Peskov said.
