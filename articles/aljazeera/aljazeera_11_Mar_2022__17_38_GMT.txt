Russia targeting hospitals schools in Ukraine: UK envoy Barbara Woodward the UK’s ambassador to the UN has accused Russia of committing war crimes in Ukraine. “Russia is invading Ukraine in violation of international law. Russia is killing hundreds of civilians through indiscriminate shelling of Ukrainian cities” she said on Friday. “It is using cluster munitions and thermobaric rockets weapons designed to inflict maximum damage wherever they are deployed. It is targeting hospitals and schools and committing war crimes.”
