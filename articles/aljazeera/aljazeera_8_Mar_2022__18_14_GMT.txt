McDonald's LOreal shut down operations works in Russia Fast-food giant McDonald’s and cosmetics company L’Oreal have joined a growing tide of companies pulling out of Russia following the invasion of Ukraine. McDonald's said it was temporarily closing its 850 restaurants in Russia and pausing all operations in the market. “We cannot ignore the needless human suffering unfolding in Ukraine” the company said ina statement. LOreal which has 2200 employees in the country also closed its stores and e-commerce sites. “We strongly condemn the Russian invasion and the war in Ukraine which is causing so much suffering to the Ukrainian people” the French company said in a statement.
