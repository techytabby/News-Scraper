Analyst: Russia yet to bring full military force to Ukraine Military Analyst Mason Clark of the Institute for the Study of War explains why Russia has not used its full military force against Ukraine.""It’s frankly miraculous that they have allowed the Ukrainian Air Force to remain operational ... when they should have theoretically had the capabilities to ground it very early on in operations” he told Al Jazeera. “So a lot of those higher-end Russian capabilities that we would expect we haven't actually seen used yet. It doesn’t of course mean that they won't ever be used and we are beginning to see some indications of Russians using greater tactical airpower” he said. “And of course as is rightly getting a lot of attention using possible thermobaric munitions and rocket artillery against civilian targets in Kharkiv and elsewhere.” Watch the video below for more.
