Zelenskyy says child has died from dehydration in besieged Mariupol Ukraine’s president says a child has died from dehydration in Mariupol amid Russia’s offensive. “In 2022 from dehydration” Zelenskyy said in a video address likening Ukraine’s humanitarian crisis to the one created by Nazi Germany during World War II. Mariupol has been heavily bombarded by Russian forces. The city has had no water power or heating supplies for days. Al Jazeera was unable to independently verify the reported death.
