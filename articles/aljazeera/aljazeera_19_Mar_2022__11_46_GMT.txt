Russia China cooperation will only get stronger: Lavrov Cooperation between Russia and China will only become stronger in the current circumstances the Interfax news agency quoted Russian foreign minister Sergey Lavrov as saying. “This cooperation will get stronger because at a time when the West is blatantly undermining all the foundations on which the international system is based of course we — as two great powers — need to think how to carry on in this world” Lavrov was quoted as saying.
