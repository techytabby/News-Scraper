Mosque sheltering 80 civilians shelled in Mariupol A mosque in the southeastern Ukrainian port city of Mariupol where 80 civilians were taking shelter has been shelled by Russian forces Ukraine’s foreign ministry said. “The mosque of Sultan Suleiman the Magnificent and his wife Roxolana (Hurrem Sultan) in Mariupol was shelled by Russian invaders. More than 80 adults and children are hiding there from the shelling including citizens of Turkey” the ministry wrote on its Twitter account.
