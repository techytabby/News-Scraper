Ukraine negotiator says talks with Russia will continue on Tuesday Talks between Ukraine and Russia have paused and will continue on Tuesday one of Ukraine’s negotiators said on Twitter. “A technical pause has been taken in the negotiations until tomorrow. For additional work in the working subgroups and clarification of individual definitions. Negotiations continue” Mykhailo Podolyak said.
