Ukraine to start 2022 spring sowing in coming days Ukraine plans to start sowing spring grains in the coming days and has enough grain in stocks to ensure the population has enough bread said Deputy Agriculture Minister Taras Vysotskiy. He said the country which consumes 8 million tonnes of wheat a year had 6 million tonnes in stocks. Stocks also include 15 million tonnes of corn and a five year supply of sunflower oil.
