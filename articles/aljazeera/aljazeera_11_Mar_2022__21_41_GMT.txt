US sanctions Russians over support for N Korea weapons programme The US has announced economic sanctions against two Russian individuals and three entities over their support for North Korea’s weapons programme. The sanctions target “a network of Russia-based individuals and entities complicit in helping the DPRK procure components for its unlawful ballistic missile systems” the treasury under-secretary for terrorism and financial intelligence Brian Nelson said in a statement referring to North Korea’s official name the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea.
