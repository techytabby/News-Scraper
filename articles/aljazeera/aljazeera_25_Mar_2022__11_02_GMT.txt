Growing evidence of Mariupol mass graves UN official says The head of the UN’s human rights team in Ukraine says that monitors have received increasing information on mass graves in Mariupol including one that appeared to hold 200 bodies. “We have got increasing information on mass graves that are there” Matilda Bogner told journalists by video link from Ukraine saying some of the evidence came from satellite images.
