Ukraine foreign minister says Iran is ‘against the war’ Ukrainian Foreign Minister Dmytro Kuleba has said Iran does not support Russia’s war in Ukraine after a call with his Iranian counterpart Hossein Amirabdollahian. “Tran is against the war in Ukraine supports a peaceful solution. I asked to convey my message in Moscow: Russia must stop bombing civilians commit to the ceasefire and withdraw from Ukraine” Kuleba wrote on Twitter. Amirabdollahian is expected to visit Moscow on Tuesday.
