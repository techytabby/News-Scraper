Russia accused of using cluster munitions incendiary weapons Oleksandra Matviichuk head of the Center for Civil Liberties in Kyiv said the group has gathered evidence of Russian forces using unguided bombs cluster munitions and incendiary weapons. “That’s why we have so [many] casualties among civilian populations” Matviichuk told Al Jazeera. She added that the centre also has gathered evidence of deliberate Russian bombings of civilian infrastructure and humanitarian corridors along which civilians are seeking to leave conflict areas. Russia has denied it targets civilians.
