White House dismisses Russia's claims of US involvement in biological warfare labs The White House has said Russia’s claims about alleged US involvement in biological weapons labs and chemical weapons development in Ukraine were false. “We took note of Russia’s false claims about alleged US biological weapons labs and chemical weapons development in Ukraine” White House press secretary Jen Psaki said in a series of tweets.
