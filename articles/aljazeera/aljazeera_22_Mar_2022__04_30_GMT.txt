Russian forces blocking access to the Sea of Azov: Ukraine army Ukraine’s military says Russian forces now hold the land corridor with occupied Crimea and are blocking access to the Sea of Azov. The Russian ground offensive remains largely stalled but its air forces are continuing to bomb infrastructure in Kyiv Chernihiv Kharkiv and in the Donetsk regions it said. Ukrainian troops have repulsed 13 Russian attacks and killed 300 Russian soldiers it added. Al Jazeera could not verify the claims.
