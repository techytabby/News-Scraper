Ukraine presidential adviser says talks with Russia going on ‘right now’ Ukrainian presidential adviser Oleksiy Arestovych has said talks between Ukraine and Russia are ongoing with the situation around the besieged city of Mariupol a particular focus for Ukrainian authorities. “Talks are continuing right now” he said in an interview on national television. Arestovych added Ukraine had enough troops deployed in Mariupol to prevent its capture by encircling Russian forces.
