Kyiv still supplied with power heat and water deputy mayor says Phone connections and heat water and power supplies are working normally in Kyiv the city’s deputy mayor has said. Mykola Povoroznyk added in televised remarks that authorities were working to evacuate many more people from the bombarded communities of Irpin Bucha and Hostomel all of which sit in the Kyiv region to the capital. “We have places to house [refugees] we have trains to send people west” Povoroznyk said.
