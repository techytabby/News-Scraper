NATO invites Ukraine to its next summit in early April NATO announced Tuesday that foreign ministers will convene at its headquarters in Brussels on April 6 and 7 — and that Ukraine is one of the countries invited There will be two sessions on 07 April. The Ministers of Foreign Affairs of Australia Finland Georgia Japan the Republic of Korea New Zealand Sweden and Ukraine as well as the High Representative of the European Union for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy/Vice- President of the European Commission are invited to join the first session"" NATO said in a statement.
