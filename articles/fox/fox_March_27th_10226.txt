Moscow says Ukrainian city Lviv hit with cruise missiles Russia's defence ministry said Sunday high-precision cruise missiles hit the western Ukrainian city of Lviv. The armed forces of the Russian Federation continue offensive actions as part of the special military operation.’ the ministry's spokesman Igor Konashenkov said in a statement. Long-range missiles hit a fuel depot near Lviv and cruise missiles struck a plat that was being used by Ukrainian forces to repair anti- aircraft systems and radar stations the ministry said.
