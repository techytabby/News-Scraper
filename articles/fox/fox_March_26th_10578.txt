Ukraine war: Kyiv transforms surgical hospital into battleground medical facility Russia's bloody invasion of Ukraine has forced the staff to transform Kyiv's largest hospital Clinical Hospital #8 into a battleground medical facility according to a recent Wall Street Journal report. Dermatologists and cardiologists now assist with triaging wounded patients arriving from artillery and rocket barrages as the hospital sits within 10 miles of the battleground the paper noted. The staff are numb to the air raid sirens because they are so frequent to take seriously anymore and don't wear face masks because of the constant background noise of fighting as they try to talk to communicate per the Journal “Of course we are not what we used to be but neither is the rest of the country"" said Dr. Igor Khomenko the hospital director.
