US official says Russian troops are redeploying not withdrawing from Kyiv AUS. official tells Fox News’ Mark Meredith Tuesday that ‘any movement of Russian forces from around Kyiv is a redeployment not a withdrawal and the world should be prepared for a major offensive against other areas of Ukraine. “No one should be fooled by Russia's announcements; the official said. ""It also does not mean the threat to Kyiv is over. Russia has failed in its objective of capturing Kyiv and failed in its objective of subjugating all of Ukraine but it can stil inflict massive brutality on the country including Kyiv.” Russia announced earlier today that it was withdrawing forces from the Kyiv and Chemihiv regions in hopes of laying the groundwork for a peace deal with Ukraine.
