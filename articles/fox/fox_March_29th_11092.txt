Funeral held for Fox News cameraman Pierre Zakrzewski in Ireland A funeral service was held on Tuesday for veteran Fox News cameraman Pierre Zakrzewski who was killed while reporting on the ground in war-tom Ukraine. FOX News Media CEO Suzanne Scott told staff that a small group from the company would be attending the service held at the Church of Our Lady of Perpetual Succour in Dublin suburb of Foxrock. Over 100 of Pierre's colleagues and loved ones from around the world wrote messages in an online condolence book.
