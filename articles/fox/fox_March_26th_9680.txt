Kyiv 35-hour curfew announced Kyiv Mayor Vitali Klitschko announced a curfew will remain in effect from 8 p.m. local time Saturday to 7 a.m. Monday. Local residents can leave their homes only to move to bomb shelters. Shops pharmacies gas stations and even public transit will not operate during this time.
