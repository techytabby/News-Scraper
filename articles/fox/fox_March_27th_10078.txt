Russia and Ukraine to start next round of live negotiations in Turkey in the coming days Ukrainian and Russian officials will meet on Monday in Istanbul for the next round of in-person peace talks a Ukrainian negotiator said Sunday. Turkish President Recep Tayyip Erdogan spoke with Russian President Vladimir Putin on Sunday to discuss the negotiations. Stressing the need for the immediate establishment of ceasefire and peace between Russia and Ukraine as well as the amelioration of the humanitarian situation in the region President [Erdogan] said that [Turkey] would continue to lend every kind of support regarding the process’ Turkish Presidency's communications office said Sunday.
