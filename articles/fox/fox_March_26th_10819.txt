Russia: Biden's comment that Putin ‘cannot remain in power' is ‘alarming’ Kremlin spokesman Dmitry Peskov said Monday that a remark made by President Joe Biden over the weekend about Vladimir Putin in which Biden said ""for God's sake this man cannot remain in power” is a ""statement that is certainly alarming’ Reuters reports. “We will continue to track the statements of the U.S. president in the most attentive way"" Peskov said.
