(AP Photo/Andrew Marienko File) About half of the population of the besieged eastern city of Kharkiv has fled as food and other essentials have started to dwindle. Kharkiv has been under siege by Russian forces since the start of the invasion with relentless shelling that has forced people to sleep in metro stations and in basements.
