Russia praises Serbia for making this 'smart choice’  Russian Foreign Minister Sergey Lavrov speaks Friday in Moscow Russia (AP/Alexandet Zemlianichenko) Russia's foreign minister Sergey Lavrov on Monday praised Serbia for refusing to impose sanctions on Moscow for its invasion of Ukraine We deeply respect the Serbian people Serbian culture Serbian history and commitment to traditional friends” Lavrov told a group of Serbian journalists in a video conference according to the Associated Press. “We are sure that they will continue to make smart choices in this situation” Although Serbia voted in favor of a UN resolution condemning Russia's invasion Belgrade has refused to join the United States and the European Union in imposing wide ranging sanctions against Moscow.
