Japan moves embassy operations from Kyiv to Lviv From CNN's Emiko Jozuka in Tokyo Japan will temporarily close its embassy operations in the Ukrainian capital Kyiv on Wednesday and move its operations to a temporary liaison office in Lviv the country’s foreign ministry spokesman said on Wednesday. The spokesperson added that Japan would ensure the safety of about 120 Japanese residents in Ukraine and support their evacuation from the country at the temporary liaison office in Lviv.
