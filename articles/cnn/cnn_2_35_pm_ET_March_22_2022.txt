At least 1 killed as Russian missile attack destroys Ukrainian train station official says From CNN's Khrystyna Bondarenko and Ivan Watson A Russian missile hit and destroyed the Paviohrad-2 train station in Pavlohrad Dnipropetrovsk says Valentin Reznichenko the Regional Head of War and Civil Administration. The strike killed one person derailed 15 freight cars and destroyed a stretch of track Reznichenko said. Station operations have been suspended indefinitely he added. Pavlohrad is an important crossroads for Ukraine’s railway system. It is a railroad cargo hub and transport link to several eastern regions which are active frontlines in Russia’s war with Ukraine. oO
