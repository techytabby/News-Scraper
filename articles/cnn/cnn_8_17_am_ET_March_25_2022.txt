Ukraine likely conducted a successful attack against Russian ships US assesses From CNN's Oren Liebermann The US has assessed that Ukraine likely did conduct a successful attack against Russian ships in Berdiansk according to a defense official. It's unclear however what type of weapon or weapons were used in the attack. The assessment echoes a similar statement from the British Ministry of Defence which said that Ukrainian forces have attacked “high-value targets” in Russian-occupied areas of Ukraine including a landing ship and ammunition depots at Berdiansk. Earlier Thursday Ukraine said they destroyed a landing ship docked at the Berdiansk port on the Sea of Azov. Video from the city showed plumes of smoke pouring out of the dock area as well as multiple explosions. On Friday Ukraine's armed forces named the Russian ship they said they attacked and destroyed in Berdiansk as the ""Saratov."" In earlier reporting the ship was named as the ""Orsk.
