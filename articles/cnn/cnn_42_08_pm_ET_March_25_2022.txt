Ukrainian army says Russian general has been killed in Kherson fighting From CNN's Yulla Kesaieva in Lviv The Ukrainian army says its forces have killed Russian Lt. Gen. Yakov Rezantsev during fighting in Chornobaiivka in the Kherson region in Ukraine's south. The army said Rezantsez was commander of the 49th Combined Arms Army of the Southern Military District of the Russian Federation. Russia's defense ministry has not commented on the Ukrainian claim.
