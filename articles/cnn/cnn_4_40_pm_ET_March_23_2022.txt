Biden arrives in Brussels for high-stakes crisis talks From CNN's Kevin Liptak President Joe Biden and Belgian Prime Minister Alexander de Croo left speak with others after arriving at Brussels National Airport Wednesday March 23. (Evan Vucci/AP) Us President Joe Biden has arrived in Brussels for a set of emergency summits meant to address Russia's war in Ukraine. Air Force One landed at the Brussels airport at 9:03 p.m. local time after a roughly seven-hour flight from Washington. Biden is expected to be greeted by Belgium's prime minister at the airport.
