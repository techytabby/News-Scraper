(Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky/Facebook) Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky said evacuation corridors did not work Wednesday as the Russian military did not stop shelling. “We are ready to take people out and send humanitarian aid. But we can't expose people to shelling on the road” he said in a video message posted to Facebook early Thursday. Zelenksy said residents who previously escaped from the besieged southeastern city of Mariupol are being moved to safer areas. “We are taking away Mariupol residents who managed to escape to Berdyansk. We are taking them to Zaporizhzhia. In total more than 6000 Mariupol residents were transported in one day more than 2000 of them are children"" he said However Zelensky said the Russian military tried to disrupt that movement too firing on the section of road between Vasylivka and Kamyanske in the Zaporizhzhia region.
