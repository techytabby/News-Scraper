(Sergey Bobok/Af Getty Images) About 1000 residential buildings have been destroyed in Kharkiv Ukraine's second-largest city Mayor thor Terehov said Wednesday. The city which is regarded as a key target for Russian President Vladimir Putin's invading forces and has sustained weeks of heavy assault sits just 30 kilometers (about 18 miles) from the Russian border.
