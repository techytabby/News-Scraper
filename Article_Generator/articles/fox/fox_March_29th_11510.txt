Russia accuses US allies of cyberattacks Russia's Foreign Ministry on Tuesday is accusing the United States and its allies of ""undertaking a massive cyber-operation’ against Moscow. The ministry claimed attacks include stealing Russians’ personal data putting pressure on the economy and spreading “fake information” about the Russian military. It also said the U.S. and other NATO members had trained Ukrainian hackers and blamed what it said was an effort by Ukraine to recruit international hackers.
