Russians destroy laboratory at Chernobyl nuclear power plant Russian troops destroyed a laboratory that helped improve management of radioactive waste at Chernoby!'s nuclear power plant the Ukrainian government said Tuesday. The laboratory contained “highly active samples and samples of radionuclides that are now in the hands of the enemy which we hope will harm itself and not the civilized world” the Ukrainian state agency responsible for the Chernobyl exclusion zone said in its statement. The laboratory was built in 2016 with the support of the European Commission at a cost of 6 million euros Forest fires also broke out near the plant on Monday causing concems that it could help spread radiation Russia took control of plant soon after invading a month ago The Associated Press contributed to this report.
