Secretary Blinken’s Call with Croatian Prime Minister Plenkovic Sec. of State Antony Blinken spoke today with Croatian Prime Minister Andrej Plenkovic reaffirming both countries’ support for Ukraine in the face of Russia's continued aggression. Blinken also thanked Prime Minister Plenkovic and his government for supporting Ukraine including providing security assistance and accepting refugees and for Croatia's active role in NATO according to a readout from the U.S. Department of State.
