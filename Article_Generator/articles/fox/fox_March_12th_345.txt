Russian tries taking over Zaporizhzhia nuclear plant: Ukraine official Russian officials attempted to enter and take operational control of Ukraine's Zaporizhzhia nuclear power plant Reuters reported. Ten officials from Rosatom a Russian state nuclear company unsuccessfully tried to enter the plant and take control of operations. Petro Kotin the head of Ukrainian energy nuclear company Energoatom said in a televised interview that “around 500 Russian soldiers with automatic weapons” remained on the plant's grounds creating a “bad psychological state” for the staff working there.
