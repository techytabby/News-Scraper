NATO chief warns Russian might use chemical weapons in Ukraine NATO Secretary General Jens Stoltenberg speaks during a shared panel with Ukrainian Prime Minister Denys Shmyhal via online t the Antalya Diplomacy Forum an international relations forum in Antalya Turkey Friday March 11 2022. NATO Secretary-General Jens Stoltenberg warned on Sunday that Russia might use chemical weapons in Ukraine. In recent days we have heard absurd claims about chemical and biological weapons laboratories” he told German newspaper Welt am Sonntag according to Reuters. “Now that these false claims have been made we must remain vigilant because it is possible that Russia itself could plan chemical weapons operations under this fabrication of lies. That would be a war crime"" he added. Posted by Brie Stimson oe
