Sheltering in a hospital basement Ukrainian kids long for home Children who are too sick to go home or flee the capital are sheltering from Russian missiles in a Kyiv hospital. Nadia Tymoshchuk 14 is eager for yet another round of cancer treatment to end so she can go home to her pet turtle and hug her brother and sister.
