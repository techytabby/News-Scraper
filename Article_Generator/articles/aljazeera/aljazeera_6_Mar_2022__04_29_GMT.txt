Ukraine official hopeful of humanitarian corridor out of Kharkiv The head of the Ukrainian delegation for talks with Russia is hopeful a humanitarian corridor out of the eastern city of Kharkiv could open on Sunday. “God willing” there will be a corridor on Sunday negotiator David Arachamija wrote on Facebook responding to a comment that he should listen and agree to a ceasefire with Russia. The commenter was from Kharkiv and said she had experienced “10 days of hell”. Ukrainian and Russian representatives last met in western Belarus on Thursday and agreed to have humanitarian corridors in place.
