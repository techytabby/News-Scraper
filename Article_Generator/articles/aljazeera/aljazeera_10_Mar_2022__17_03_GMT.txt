France's Macron says bombing of Mariupol hospital ‘disgraceful’ Russia’s bombing of a maternity hospital in the besieged Ukrainian city of Mariupol was “a disgraceful act of war” French President Emmanuel Macron has said. France condemned the attack “in the firmest possible terms” Macron told reporters as EU leaders gathered outside Paris to discuss their response to the conflict. “The clear objective was to kill civilians especially women and children” he said.
