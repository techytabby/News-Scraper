UK's Mothercare suspends all business in Russia United Kingdom baby products retailer Mothercare has said all its business in Russia including the shipment of all products has been suspended. The company said its local partner in Russia will be pausing operations in about 120 stores and online. Russia represents around 20 to 25 percent of Mothercare’s worldwide retail sales and was previously expected to contribute around 0.5 million pounds ($0.7m) per month to group profit.
