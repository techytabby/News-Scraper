Russian attacks hit schools cathedral in Kharkiv Russian military attacks hit three schools and a cathedral in the northeastern town of Kharkiv local media reported adding that several shops near the city council building were also damaged. In Okhtyrka dozens of “residential buildings” were destroyed as a result of artillery blasts the reports said. No injuries have been reported.
