Russian forces interfering at Ukraine nuclear plant: IAEA Russian forces have tightened their control on the Zaporizhzhia nuclear power plant since seizing it on Friday the United Nations nuclear watchdog has warned. The head of the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) Rafael Grossi said he was “extremely concerned” after Ukraine’s nuclear regulator informed the agency that staff members at the plant the largest in Ukraine are now required to seek approval from Russian forces for any operation even maintenance.
