US embassy in Ukraine calls power plant attack ‘war crime’ The US embassy in Ukraine has said that attacking a nuclear power plant is a war crime after Russia seized a Ukrainian nuclear facility that is the biggest in Europe. The statement on the embassy’s Twitter account went further than any US characterisation of Russia’s actions in Ukraine since it launched its invasion on February 24. “It is a war crime to attack a nuclear power plant.
