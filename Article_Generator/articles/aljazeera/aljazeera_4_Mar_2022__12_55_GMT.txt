Q&A: Moscow's ‘lone climate protester’ confronts war on Ukraine Arshak Makichyan is a prominent activist who has been protesting in the Russian capital for years warning people against the dangers of climate change. His efforts have seen him nicknamed Moscow’s “lone climate protester” as a law in Russia bans unsanctioned protests of more than one person.
