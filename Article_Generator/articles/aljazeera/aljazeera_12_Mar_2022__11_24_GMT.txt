Chernihiv governor says city under heavy attack Ukraine’s northern city of Chernihiv is still facing heavy Russian shelling the region’s governor Viacheslav Chaus said. Ina video address Chaus said Russian troops were targeting civilian infrastructure “where there is no and has never been any military”. “The peaceful population is dying and there are many injured” he said. Footage from a local television station showed large-scale destruction to several buildings and a stadium in the city.
