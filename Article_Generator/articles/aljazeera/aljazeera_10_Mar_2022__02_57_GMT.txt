UNICEF says at least 37 children killed in Ukraine At least 37 children have been killed and 50 injured “in less than two weeks” UNICEF executive director Catherine Russell has said adding that more than one million children were forced to flee. “Attacks against civilians and civilian infrastructure — including hospitals water and sanitation systems and schools — are unconscionable and must stop immediately” Russell said ina statement. “The children of Ukraine desperately need peace.”
