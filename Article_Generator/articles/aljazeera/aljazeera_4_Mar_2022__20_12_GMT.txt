Russia blocks access to Facebook amid war with Ukraine The country’s communications regulator says it blocked Meta Platforms Inc’s Facebook in response to what it said were restrictions of access to Russian media on its platform more than a week after Moscow invaded its neighbour Ukraine. The regulator Roskomnadzor said there had been 26 cases of discrimination against Russian media by Facebook since October 2020 with access restricted to state-backed channels like RT and the RIA news agency.
