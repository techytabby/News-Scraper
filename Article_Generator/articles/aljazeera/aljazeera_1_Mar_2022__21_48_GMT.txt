Ukrainian refugees stream into Hungary More than 85000 people have crossed from Ukraine into Hungary since the start of Russia’s invasion. Along the 135km (84-mile) frontier refugees are met with hot tea locally made sandwiches and logistical help — calls to embassies free ticket booking and even entertainment for children.
