US mulls possible ban on oil imports from Russia The US government is consulting with its European allies on a potential import ban of oil from Russia. “We are now talking to our European partners and allies to look ina coordinated way at the prospect of banning the import of Russian oil” Blinken told CNN. The debate also revolved around “making sure that there is still an appropriate supply of oil on world markets” Blinken said. “That’s a very active discussion as we speak.” US President Joe Biden has not explicitly ruled out such a move.
