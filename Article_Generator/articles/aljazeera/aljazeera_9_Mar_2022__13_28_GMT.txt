UN nuclear watchdog says Chernobyl power cut has no critical impact on safety The loss of power at the Chernobyl plant does not have any critical impact on safety at the site the UN’s nuclear watchdog says. The International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) tweeted that the “heat load of spent fuel storage pool and volume of cooling water at Chernobyl Nuclear Power Plant [are] sufficient for effective heat removal without [the] need for electrical supply”.
