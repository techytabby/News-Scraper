War in Ukraine tests shaky US-China relations In recent days several top officials in the US have tried to discourage China from backing Russia in its war in Ukraine amid reports that Moscow has requested military assistance from Beijing. While Chinese officials have downplayed the reports experts say the US's public pressure campaign on China could define an already shaky relationship between the two nations for years to come. “This has the potential to be a turning point in US-China relations” Robert Ross a political science professor at Boston College told Al Jazeera.
