Thousands rally in Paris in solidarity with Ukraine Several thousand protesters took to the streets of Paris to show their support for Ukraine. Protesters lined the streets of the French capital holding up banners and waving Ukrainian flags as they called on Russia to withdraw its troops from Ukrainian territory. Among them was Ukraine’s ambassador to France Vadym. Omelchenko. “Right now every minute in Ukraine Russian armed forces are killing civilians and children” Omelchenko said as he marched alongside other protesters.
