Russia: More than 1100 detained at Ukraine war protests More than 1100 people in cities across Russia have been detained at protests on Sunday against Moscow’s military operation in Ukraine a protest monitor said. The OVD-Info group said by 2:20pm Moscow time (11:20 GMT) on Sunday 1103 people had been detained across 35 cities bringing the total number of demonstrators detained to 9472 since February 24 when President Putin ordered troops into Ukraine to carry out a “special operation”.
