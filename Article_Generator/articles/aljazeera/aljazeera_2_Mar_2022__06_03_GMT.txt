Google blocks RT Sputnik from Play app store in Europe Alphabet Inc’s Google has said it has blocked mobile apps connected to RT and Sputnik from its Play store in line with an earlier move to remove the Russian state publishers from its news-related features. A number of tech companies have limited distribution and advertising tools to Russian news outlets in recent days as the European Commission readies a ban on them out of concern that they are spreading misinformation about the war in Ukraine.
