Ukrainian presidential adviser says Ukraine war is at a crossroads An adviser to Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy has said that the war in Ukraine is at a crossroads that could lead to an agreement at talks with Russia or a new Russian offensive. “We are at a crossroads. Either we will agree at the current talks or the Russians will make a second attempt [at an offensive] and then there will be talks again” adviser Oleksiy Arestovych said.
