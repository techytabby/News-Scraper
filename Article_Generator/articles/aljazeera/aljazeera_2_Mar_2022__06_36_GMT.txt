At least 21 killed 112 wounded in shelling of Kharkiv: Ukrainian official At least 21 people have been killed and 112 wounded in shelling in the eastern Ukrainian city of Kharkiv in the last 24 hours regional governor Oleg Synegubov has said. The authorities said Russian missile attacks hit the centre of Ukraine’s second-largest city including residential areas and the regional administration building.
