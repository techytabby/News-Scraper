Five Western countries condemn Russia's role in Syria Ukraine conflicts France Germany Italy the United Kingdom and the United States have issued a joint statement commemorating the 11th anniversary of the conflict in Syria and condemning Russia’s role both there and in its invasion of Ukraine. “The coincidence of this year’s anniversary with the appalling Russian aggression against Ukraine which constitutes a breach of exceptional gravity to international law and the UN Charter highlights Russia's brutal and destructive behaviour in both conflicts” the statement released by the German foreign ministry said.
