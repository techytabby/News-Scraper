Bloomberg News temporarily suspending work in Russia Bloomberg News has said it was temporarily suspending the work of its journalists inside Russia citing a new law that could mean jail for those found to be intentionally spreading “fake” news. “The change to the criminal code which seems designed to turn any independent reporter into a criminal purely by association makes it impossible to continue any semblance of normal journalism inside the country” Bloomberg Editor-in-Chief John Micklethwait said in a statement.
