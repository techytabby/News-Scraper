Russian forces head toward Zaporizhzhia nuclear power plant: Local mayor The mayor of the Ukrainian town of Energodar said a column of Russian troops was headed toward the nearby Zaporizhzhia nuclear power plant Europe’s largest late on Thursday. Earlier the Ukrainian authorities reported that Russian troops were stepping up efforts to seize the plant in southeast Ukraine and had entered Energodar with tanks. “Loud shots can be heard in the town” said Mayor Dmytro Orlov in an online post.
