ICC prosecutor opens Ukraine investigation The prosecutor of the ICC has confirmed he will immediately open an investigation into possible war crimes committed in Ukraine following a request by 39 of the court’s member states. “These referrals enable my office to proceed with opening an investigation into the situation in Ukraine from 21 November 2013 onwards encompassing any past and present allegations of war crimes crimes against humanity or genocide committed on any part of the territory of Ukraine by any person” Khan said.
