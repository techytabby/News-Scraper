16 killed in Mykolaiv attack: ministry The number of casualties from Tuesday's attack on the regional administration building in the southern city of Mykolaiv has risen to 16 people according to Ukraine’s State Emergency Service. The service said in a Telegram post that rescuers had worked all night looking for bodies and survivors in the debris of the nine-storey building which had a gaping hole after being struck by Russian forces. Al Jazeera was unable to independently verify the figures provided. Reporting by Mansur Mirovalev in Vinnytsia Ukraine.
