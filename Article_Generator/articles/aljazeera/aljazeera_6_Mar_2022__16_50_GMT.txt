Zhytomyr under heavy shelling as civilians pledge to fight Civilians in Zhytomyr 150 kilometres (93 miles) west of Kyiv have pledged to remain and fight despite heavy Russian shelling that devastated dozens of buildings and killed several people including children. “It’s difficult for civilians to understand what was the purpose of these strikes” Al Jazeera’s Step Vaessen reported noting that the town is a few hundreds meters away from a military base. “The anger here is unbearable” she said as she walked through the rubble of one of the three residential areas that were flattened by the attacks. “People are really defiant ... they are saying they are not going anywhere and if the Russians come we will fight them” she added.
