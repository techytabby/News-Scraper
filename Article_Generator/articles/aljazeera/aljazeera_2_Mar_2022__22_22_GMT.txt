EU bars 7 Russian banks from SWIFT but not those in energy The EU says it is excluding seven Russian banks from the SWIFT messaging system — but did not include those handling energy payments. Russia’s second-largest bank VTB Bank Otkritie Novikombank Promsvyazbank Bank Rossiya Sovcombank and VEB will each be given 10 days to wind down their SWIFT operations the EU said in its official journal. SWIFT said that it would disconnect the seven Russian banks from their network on March 12 as required by EU regulations.
