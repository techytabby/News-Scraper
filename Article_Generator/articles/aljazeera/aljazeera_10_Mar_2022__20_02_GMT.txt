Russia agrees to allow repairs on power lines near Chernobyl The Russian defence ministry has said it agreed to allow a Ukrainian repair team to access power lines in the area around the Chernobyl nuclear power station Interfax news agency reported. The UN’s nuclear watchdog said this week it had lost communications with Chernoby] and another nuclear power station at Zaporizhzhia describing the situation as concerning. IAEA chief Rafael Grossi was in Turkey to meet Russia and Ukraine’s foreign ministers separately. “I am quite encouraged on one important thing: Ukraine and the Russian Federation want to work with us” Grossi told a news conference.
