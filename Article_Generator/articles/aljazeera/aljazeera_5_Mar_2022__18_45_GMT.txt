UK accuse Russia of Mariupol ceasefire plan to reset forces The United Kingdom said on Saturday that Russia’s proposed ceasefire in the Ukrainian city of Mariupol was probably an attempt to deflect international condemnation while giving itself a chance to reset its forces for a renewed offensive. “By accusing Ukraine of breaking the agreement Russia is likely seeking to shift responsibility for current and future civilian casualties in the city” the UK defence ministry said in an intelligence update.
