IPC president calls for peace at Winter Paralympics opening ceremony The president of the International Paralympic Committee (IPC) has made an impassioned call for peace in Ukraine during the opening ceremony of the Winter Paralympics in Beijing. “T am horrified at what is taking place in the world right now. The 21st century is a time for dialogue and diplomacy not war and hate” Andrew Parsons said in an address at the Chinese capital's Bird’s Nest Stadium. “The world must be a place for sharing not dividing” he added.
