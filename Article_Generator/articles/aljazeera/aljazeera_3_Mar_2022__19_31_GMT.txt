PHOTOS: Ukrainians flee to Romania to escape Russia's invasion Since Russian forces launched an attack on February 24 more than one million people have fled Ukraine as per the UNHCR. According to Romania's Prime Minister Nicolae Ciuca as of Thursday morning 118461 Ukrainian refugees had entered neighbouring Romania. Many passed through to other countries but Ciuca said at least 46435 remain in Romania. At the border crossing in Isaccea some arrived in Romania on foot pushing trollers or baby buggies others managed to pass in their cars packing the little they could take in their escape from war.
