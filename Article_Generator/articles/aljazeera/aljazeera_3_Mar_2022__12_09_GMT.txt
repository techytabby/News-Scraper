Second round of Ukraine-Russia talks to be held today Ukrainian official says A Ukrainian delegation will meet with Russian officials later today for a second round of negotiations Ukrainian presidential adviser Mykhailo Podolyak has said. Podolyak confirmed in a statement online that the Ukrainian delegation was on its way to the discussions via helicopter. Podolyak’s remarks came after the Belarusian state news agency Belta quoted chief Russian negotiator Vladimir Medinsky as saying the second round of talks would start in the coming hours.
