Air raid: 17 wounded at Mariupol children’s hospital says official A Russian air raid on a children’s hospital in Mariupol has injured 17 people according to preliminary figures a local official said. “So far there are 17 wounded personnel of the hospital” Pavlo Kyrylenko the head of the southeastern Donetsk region said in a video posted on Facebook.
