Miles-long Russian convoy appears stalled near Kyiv: US official An enormous Russian military convoy is lumbering towards Kyiv raising fears of an imminent uptick in fighting there. A senior US defence official told reporters the convoy appeared to stall on Tuesday however due to a number of factors including the Ukrainian resistance that the Russian troops have faced. “We also believe that a part of the stall could be — and I emphasise the word ‘could’ — could be a result of their own self-determined sort of pause in operations — that they are possibly regrouping rethinking reevaluating” the official said on condition of anonymity.
