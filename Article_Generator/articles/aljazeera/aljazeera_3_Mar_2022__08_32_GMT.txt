China says more than 3000 of its citizens have left Ukraine More than 3000 Chinese citizens have been safely relocated from Ukraine to neighbouring countries amid Russia’s offensive the country’s Foreign Ministry Spokesman Wang Wenbin has told a daily news briefing. Unlike many countries China did not tell its roughly 6000 citizens in Ukraine to leave the country in the days leading up to Moscow's incursion. Instead Beijing announced evacuation plans soon after the assault had begun.
