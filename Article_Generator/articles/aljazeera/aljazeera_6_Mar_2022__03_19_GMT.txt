Ukraine says Russia wants to seize power station dam The Ukrainian army says Russian forces want to seize the dam of a key hydroelectric power station south of the capital Kyiv. Ina report issued early on Sunday the Ukrainian army said Russia planned to seize the Kaniv hydroelectric power station’s dam some 150km (93 miles) south of Kyiv on the Dnieper River. Russian forces have destroyed attacked or captured several energy facilities including Europe’s largest nuclear power plant in Zaporizhzhia.
