UN committed to scaled-up humanitarian efforts Secretary-General Antonio Guterres says the United Nations is committed to scaling up its humanitarian operations to help both those who have stayed in Ukraine and the millions who have fled. Guterres relayed the promise to Ukrainian Foreign Minister Dmytro Kuleba in a phone call on Saturday UN spokesman Stephane Dujarric said. Dujarric said the two also discussed the conditions for safely evacuating civilians including foreigners from combat zones. UN Secretary-General Antonio Guterres [File: John Minchillo/AP Photo]
