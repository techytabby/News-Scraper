Mariupol city council says children’s hospital destroyed by Russian bombing A children’s hospital in Mariupol has been destroyed by Russian air raids according to the city council. “The Russian occupying forces have dropped several bombs on the children’s hospital. The destruction is colossal” the council said in an online post. It did not provide any information about possible casualties.
