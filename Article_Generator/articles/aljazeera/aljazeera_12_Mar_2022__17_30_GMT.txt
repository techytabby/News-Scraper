‘Let them come in’: Zelenskyy President Zelenskyy has suggested Russian forces would face a fight to the death if they sought to enter the capital. “Tf they decide to carpet bomb (Kyiv) and simply erase the history of this region the history of the Kyivan Rus the history of Europe and destroy all of us then they will enter Kyiv. If that’s their goal let them come in but they will have to live on this land by themselves” he told a news conference.
