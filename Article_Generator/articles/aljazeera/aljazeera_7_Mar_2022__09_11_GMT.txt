Humanitarian corridors unlikely while Russia keeps attacking Kyiv says Kyiv has said that negotiations are taking place with Russia on establishing humanitarian corridors but warned that such escape routes are unlikely to be set up while Moscow's forces press ahead with their offensive. Oleksiy Arestovich an adviser to Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy’s chief of staff said each Ukrainian should be allowed to decide where they want to go but advised against evacuating to Russia. Moscow claims it is only targeting military infrastructure but Kyiv says Russian attacks have targeted civilian areas in several cities nationwide [Carlos Barria/Reuters]
